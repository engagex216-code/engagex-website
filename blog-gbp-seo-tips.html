15 GBP SEO Tips to Optimize Your Google Business Profile in 2026

By EngageX Team 8 min read GBP Optimization

Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is the most important local SEO asset you have. A well-optimized profile can mean the difference between appearing in the top 3 and being buried on the 4th page.

Here are 15 actionable tips to optimize your GBP for higher rankings and more customers.

1

Use Your Real Business Name

Don't keyword-stuff your business name. Google penalizes this. Use your actual business name.

✅ "Delhi Spice Restaurant"    ❌ "Delhi Spice Restaurant | Best Indian Food in Delhi"
2

Choose the Right Categories

  • Primary Category — The most relevant category (e.g., "Restaurant").
  • Secondary Categories — Up to 9 additional categories (e.g., "Indian Restaurant," "Dinner Restaurant").
Pro Tip

Use Google's category suggestions. Don't force irrelevant categories.

3

Write a Strong Business Description

  • 150-300 words — use keywords naturally.
  • Include: what you do, why you're different, key services or products, your location (if relevant).
  • Don't: keyword-stuff or add HTML/URLs.
4

Add High-Quality Photos

  • 10-20 photos minimum.
  • Types — Interior, exterior, products, team, menu, events.
  • Photo Requirements — 400x300 minimum, 720x720 preferred.
  • Update — Add new photos regularly to show activity.
5

Use All 10 Attributes

Attributes help customers know what to expect. Use them all! Examples: "Wheelchair accessible," "Free Wi-Fi," "Outdoor seating," "Parking available."

6

Add Service Areas

If you serve multiple areas, add them all. If you have a physical store, make sure your address is accurate.

7

Keep Your Hours Updated

  • Regular Hours — Keep them accurate.
  • Special Hours — Update for holidays and special events.
  • Don't — Leave your hours blank or inaccurate.
8

Link to Your Website

  • Use your primary website URL.
  • Add a phone link (for mobile users).
  • Use UTM parameters to track traffic from GBP.
9

Enable & Respond to Messaging

Google's messaging feature lets customers chat with you directly. Enable it and respond quickly (within 24 hours).

10

Add a Virtual Tour

If possible, add a 360° virtual tour. This is a strong signal to Google and helps customers explore your business before visiting.

11

Post Weekly

  • Google Posts are a ranking signal.
  • Types — Offers, Events, News, Product Updates.
  • Add — An image and a clear CTA.
  • Frequency — At least 1-2 posts per week.
12

Manage Your Q&A

  • Answer all questions — even if they're old.
  • Add FAQs — Pre-populate the Q&A section with common questions.
  • Include keywords in your answers.
13

Encourage Product Reviews

If you sell products, enable product reviews. This adds depth to your profile.

14

Monitor Your Insights

  • Search Queries — See how people find you.
  • Views — Track profile views.
  • Actions — Monitor calls, direction requests, and website clicks.
  • Use Insights to optimize based on what's working.
15

Embed a Google Map on Your Website

This is a strong local SEO signal that confirms your location to Google.

What Not to Do

❌ Keyword-stuffing — Google penalizes this
❌ Duplicate listings — avoid creating multiple listings
❌ Inconsistent NAP — keep Name, Address, Phone consistent everywhere
❌ Ignoring negative reviews — address them professionally
❌ Fake reviews — use safe, authentic providers only
