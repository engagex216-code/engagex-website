Yes. All reviews are posted from aged, real-looking accounts in a natural, staggered manner. We follow safe delivery practices to minimize any risk of removal. Our reviews have a very high retention rate.

Indian reviews are delivered within 24–72 hours. International reviews take 48–72 hours. Larger orders may be staggered over a week for safety.

Just your Google Business Profile link and the review content (or we can write it for you). That's it — no login credentials needed.
